Recipe from our customer

Anushka Shrestha, Sep 2, 2017

Ingredients

  • 2 bags Lipton® Iced Tea
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • Ice
  • Lemon slices and mint leaves (optional)

Method

  1. Brew Lipton® Iced Tea Southern Sweet Tea Family Size in 4 cups boiling water for 4 minutes (brew longer for stronger tea flavor).
  2. Remove bags and squeeze out all the goodness. Stir in sugar until dissolved. Fill pitcher with ice. Pour tea over ice and garnish with lemon and mint.
